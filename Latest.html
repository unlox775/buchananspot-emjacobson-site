Added Sept. 5, 2008:
Added a page showing a family heirloom jewelry box possibly originally belonging to Elizabeth Smith Lovell. The details of the contents lead to that conclusion. The box contained a picture frame containing three tintype pictures. The wood of the frame is similar to the wood of the box and one of the pictures in the frame (behind the other two) apprears to be that of Elizabeth Smith Lovell. The other two pictures have first names written on the back of them: Sarah and Mary. Elizabeth had two sisters named Mary and Sarah. The evidence on all this is still pretty thin, but it is possible.
