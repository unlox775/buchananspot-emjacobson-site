 LaPriel, Myron and Clora lived with their Aunt and Uncle, Delilah and Eddie Jacobson for some time after the death of their mother. These pictures were taken as they were associated with this family. Their cousin, Arvilla Jacobson (later Buchanan) is in some of the pictures as well. She always regarded these three cousins as sisters and brother.
